Exercises:

  1. Surplus Nightmare
  2. Space Invaders

Goal:

To hit a mental block and utilize a creative thinking pattern to get through it.

Surplus Nightmare

Production has fat thumbed an order for 1 million ball bearings. You cannot return them and they are taking up to much warehouse space. You need to come up with ways to get rid of them and make some money back on the order if possible.

Your team has 5 minutes to come up with 5 solutions.

Ready?

GO!

  • Ordered too many ball bearings
  • Make some money back
  • 5 minutes : 5 ideas

Surplus Nightmare: Recap

  • What ideas did you have?
  • Did you have 5 or more ideas?
  • Were you afraid of failure?
  • Did you participate?

Space Invaders

You find yourself sitting at a cluttered desk with these objects surrounding you. All of a sudden aliens land out in the parking lot. They demand to only speak to this group of people. They have informed you that they will destroy the Earth if you do not build them a famous earthling dohickey in the next 5 minutes.

Ready?

Go!

  • Aliens Invading
  • Build A Dohickey
  • 5 Minutes

Space Invaders: Recap

  • What did you build?
  • How did it please the Aliens?
    • Did you hit any mental blocks?
    • What did you do to get over them?
  • Did you participate?
